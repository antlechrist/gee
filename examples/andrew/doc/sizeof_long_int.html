sizeof long int

Remember:

#include <limits.h>

AND

#include <stdio.h>

for long int

long, signed integer; signed means positive to negative i.e.; 100 to -100, not just positive i.e.; 0 – 200.

from: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Printf

printf("%ld to %ld\n", LONG_MAX, LONG_MIN);
